PROJECT OVERVIEW
This Digital Literacy Project is designed to help students understand
and practice safe, responsible, and effective use of digital 
technologies. In today’s world, where most activities such as 
communication, learning, and transactions take place online, it is 
important to be aware of both the benefits and risks of using digital 
platforms.
The project consists of five tasks that focus on different aspects of 
digital literacy. These include creating a presentation, building a 
digital portfolio, exploring online platforms, learning professional 
email etiquette, and studying cybercrime awareness. Each task involves 
practical activities that help in developing real-life skills.
Overall, this project aims to improve digital communication, promote 
safe online behavior, and increase awareness about cyber threats. It 
encourages students to become responsible digital citizens and use 
technology in a smart and secure way.


MODULE SUMMARIES

Module 1 – Digital Literacy Basics
This module introduced the basic concept of digital literacy and its 
importance in today’s world. It explained how digital technologies 
are used in everyday activities like communication, learning, and 
online transactions. I learned about both the advantages and risks 
of using digital platforms, including issues like data privacy and 
cyber threats. The module emphasized the need to use technology 
responsibly and safely. It helped me understand that digital literacy 
is not just about using devices, but also about making informed 
decisions and being aware of online risks.


Module 2 – Building Digital Identity 
This module focused on creating and maintaining a professional digital 
identity. I learned how platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub can be used 
to showcase skills, projects, and achievements. It also highlighted 
the importance of keeping profiles updated and maintaining a positive 
online presence. I understood how recruiters may view these profiles 
and how they can impact future opportunities. This module helped me 
realize that my online presence represents me professionally, and I 
should use it carefully and effectively to build a strong digital profile.


Module 3 – Exploring Learning Platforms 
In this module, I explored different digital learning platforms and 
understood how they support skill development. I learned how platforms 
like HackerEarth provide coding challenges, practice problems, and learning 
resources. I performed activities and observed their features and benefits. 
This module helped me become more comfortable using online platforms for 
learning and improving my skills. It also showed me how digital tools can 
be used productively for academic growth and self-improvement.


Module 4 – Email Etiquette & The Basics of Professional Communication 
This module focused on understanding the basics of professional email 
communication. I learned how to write emails with a clear subject line, 
proper greeting, structured content, and a polite tone. It emphasized the 
importance of clarity and professionalism in digital communication. I 
practiced writing emails for real-life situations such as requesting an 
extension and applying for an internship. This module helped me understand 
how good communication skills can create a positive impression and avoid 
misunderstandings in academic and professional settings.


Module 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide 
This module focused on understanding cybercrime and ways to prevent it. 
I studied a case related to UPI fraud and learned how such scams happen 
step-by-step. It also explained who is usually targeted and the consequences 
of such crimes. I created a prevention checklist with practical safety tips 
and learned about reporting cybercrime through official channels. This 
module increased my awareness of online risks and the importance of staying 
alert. It encouraged me to follow safe practices and help others stay informed.
